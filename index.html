Antennae

An antenna or aerial is a metal device made to send or receive radio waves. Many electronic devices like radio, television, radar, wireless LAN, cell phone, and GPS need antennas to do their job.

Though much of our internet is provided through cables and powerlines, antennae are everywhere. These enable various wireless networking for many different purposes. The antennae are most likely for networkctelevision or radio, and those are interconnected with network.

CCTV Cameras

Surveillance cameras are video cameras used for the purpose of observing an area. They are often connected to a recording device or IP network, and may be watched by a security guard or law enforcement officer.

CCTV Camera

CCTV cameras, or closed-circuit television cameras,is increasingly becoming a common sight in our daily lives. They're found mostly anywhere.The primary purpose of these cameras is for security as well as collecting data occasionally.These cameras send information to a wider network, and by doing so, connect us to that wider network.

Powerlines

Powerlines transmit power generated at a variety of facilities and distributes it to end users, often over long distances. They provide electricity to buildings, industrial facilities, schools, and homes. And they do so every minute of every day, year-round.

Powerline

All across Wellington, you can find powerlines as they provide the power needed to ensure that the people of Wellington have access to electrical facilities or appliances.
